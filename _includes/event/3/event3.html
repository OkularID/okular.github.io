About Oceandor

Oceandor is an indie fashion brand focusing on making eco-cotton and sustainable clothing to help reducing waste in the earth. Every item bought is also donated to go green organization working on mother earth harmony of plants and animals. We are vegan and cruelty-free, and we have a purpose to create the most sustainable piece of fashion that people can wear every day. Now that we are opening our offline store, we’re inviting you to join the celebration and first-day big sale for all of our products!

Special
Guests

Daft Punk are a French electronic music duo formed in Paris in 1993 by Guy-Manuel de Homem-Christo and Thomas Bangalter. They achieved popularity in the late 1990s as part of the French house movement; they also had success in the years following, combining elements of house music with funk, techno, disco, rock, and synthpop. They have worn ornate helmets and gloves to assume robot personas in most public appearances since 1999 and rarely grant interviews or appear on television. The duo was managed from 1996 to 2008 by Pedro Winter (also known as Busy P), the head of Ed Banger Records.
